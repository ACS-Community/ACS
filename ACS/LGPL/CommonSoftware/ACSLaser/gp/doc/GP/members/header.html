GP project members
The project shall be organized as a small core team that collaborates with external developers in other projects. The core team has two main tasks: (1) provide the common GUI platform based on the NetBeans platform and (2) set up a support structure to help the external developers use the common platform and to migrate their old StOpMI based applications to the new platform. The external developers develop their project specific GUIs, and assure the link between the core team and their project.
